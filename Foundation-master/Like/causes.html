Our Programs

Housing for underprivileged families

The effect of poverty is seen in the number of families living in squalor. In their struggle for daily survival, their ability to afford good housing is far-fetched. As a result, they are exposed to adverse weather conditions, mosquito infestations and various kinds of diseases.In the past three years, the foundation has succeeded in providing ten(10) families with free housing in the Let's Meet A Need (LMAN) Housing scheme. Beneficiary communities include Ugbuwangue, Ekurede Itsekiri, Ekurede Urhobo, Okere, Pessu and Agbassa. It is our desire that even more communities would be reached and the need for good housing for underprivileged families would be met.

Register a Child in School (RACIS) project

Results gotten by applying random sampling techniques to some select communities in Delta state reveal that financial constraints constitute a major reason why most kids have not been registered in school. The RACIS project is designed to eliminate this challenge by educating underprivileged children in Delta state irrespective of their states of origin. These underprivileged kids are not just enrolled in school, but are provided with essential materials for their schooling such as uniforms, books, etc. A monitoring program has also been set up by the foundation to follow up with the progress and performance of the kids. From 2017 till date, forty eight (48) kids have been registered in school as a result of the scheme and we look forward to doing more in the coming years.


Let's Meet A Need (LMAN) End of year party

Yearly, the foundation organizes an end-of-the-year party to celebrate the successes of the year with underprivileged kids from various communities in Delta State. Last year's event had over three hundred (300) children in attendance. Fun activities like games, cultural displays and even health talks and motivational sessions highlighted the day. Gift packs containing various back-to-school materials were distributed to the children by Santa while food items were given to the parents to celebrate the festive season. Our dream is to accomplish greater things for these underprivileged ones with each passing year.

Youths & Empowerment

In the youths lies the future of any nation. Ignore them and expect a society filled with crimes and vices. Build them up and secure a future filled with greatness. In line with the mission of the foundation, it is our desire to empower young people in the various communities of the state with the needed knowledge and materials necessary to engage in a trade, practice a skill, thus creating better lives for themselves and their families.
