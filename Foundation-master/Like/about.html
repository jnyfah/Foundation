The LIKE (Let's Intiate Kids Education) foundation is a non-governmental organization born out of the desire to cause a positive change in the world and invariably, make it a better place.

It was birthed on October 2016 and runs three core programs: the Register A Child In School (RACIS) project, the Let's Meet A Need Housing (LMAN Housing) project and the Let's Empower Youths (LEY) project. An end of the year party is also organized yearly by the foundation. LIKE envisions a society where underprivileged kids are educated, youths and women are empowered.

Mission: To enhance lives of underprivileged children, youths and women in Delta state and the country at large by working closely with our donors and sponsors in educating underprivileged children, empowering youths and women.

HOW THIS WORKS

How does it work?

We have a simple, established style that is making a difference. Kids and other individuals in our program have access to:

1. A caring TEAM that inspires, encourages and supports our sponsored children.

1. A safe PLACE, LIKE also provides accomodation for families living in squalor.

3. A clear PATH out of poverty that focuses on education and empowerment through country-relevant and age-specific programs and services.

Do you believe you can make a difference in the world? Do you believe helping kids is the best way to end poverty? Then YES!
